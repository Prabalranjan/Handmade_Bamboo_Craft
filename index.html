Our Story

Handmade Bamboo Craft is a small, home-grown venture based in Assam, one of India's richest bamboo-growing regions. Every lamp, planter, and cup we make is shaped by hand using traditional weaving and carving techniques passed down through generations of local artisans.

We're just getting started — right now we ship these handmade pieces anywhere in India, and we're figuring things out as we grow. Thank you for supporting small, sustainable craft.
