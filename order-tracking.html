Order Tracking

We're a small team and don't yet have an automated tracking page — here's the fastest way to get a real answer.

Check your order status on WhatsApp

Since every order is confirmed and shipped by us directly, the quickest way to get an update is to message us on WhatsApp with your name and the date you ordered. We'll reply with your current status — packed, shipped, or out for delivery — along with the courier tracking number once it's dispatched.

Haven't heard back?

If it's been more than 2 business days since you ordered with no update, please follow up on WhatsApp or via our Contact page — we'll chase it down for you.
