Shipping & Returns

What to expect once you've placed an order.

Last updated: January 2026

Processing time

Every piece is handmade to order or packed fresh from our small batch stock, so please allow 2–4 business days to craft and pack your order before it ships.

Delivery timelines

We ship pan-India via courier. Once dispatched, delivery typically takes 5–10 business days depending on your location. We'll share the courier tracking details with you over WhatsApp as soon as your order ships.

Shipping cost

Shipping cost depends on the size, weight, and destination of your order, and will be confirmed with you over WhatsApp before your order is finalized.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged or isn't what you ordered, please message us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and packaging, and we'll sort out a replacement or refund.

Returns and exchanges

We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery for damaged, defective, or incorrect items. Because every piece is handmade, minor natural variation in grain, tone, or weave is expected and isn't eligible for return on its own.

To be eligible, items must be unused and in their original packaging. To start a return or exchange, message us via Contact or WhatsApp with your order details and photos of the item.

Refunds

Once a return is received and inspected, we'll confirm your refund or replacement over WhatsApp. Refunds are issued using the same payment method used for the order, where possible.

Questions

Reach out any time via our Contact page or WhatsApp — we're happy to help.
