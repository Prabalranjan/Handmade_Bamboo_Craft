PRODUCT TITLE:
ZenWeave 3-Light Natural Bamboo Cluster Pendant Chandelier

------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRODUCT OVERVIEW:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Bring the calming essence of organic modern design into your home with 
our ZenWeave 3-Light Natural Bamboo Pendant Chandelier. Masterfully 
handcrafted from sustainable, eco-friendly natural bamboo, this statement 
ceiling fixture features three distinct woven lantern shades tiered at 
varying heights. The intricate latticework filters bright bulb glow into 
a soft, warm, pattern-dappled ambiance that instantly cozies up any 
living space. 

Whether suspended over a minimalist dining table, highlighting a cozy 
bedroom corner, or warming up a restaurant entryway, this versatile 
cluster lamp beautifully bridges the gap between retro bohemian charm 
and Japanese rustic elegance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

* Artisanal Craftsmanship: Each shade is individually hand-woven by
