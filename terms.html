Who we are

Handmade Bamboo Craft is a small, home-grown venture based in Assam, India, making handcrafted bamboo lamps, planters, cups, and home decor. By ordering from us, you agree to the terms below.

Products

Every piece is handmade from natural bamboo using traditional techniques. Expect gentle natural variation in grain, tone, and weave between pieces — this is a feature of genuine handmade work, not a fault. Product photos are representative but may differ slightly from the piece you receive.
