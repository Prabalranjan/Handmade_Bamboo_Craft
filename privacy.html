Privacy Policy

A plain-language explanation of what we collect and why.

Last updated: January 2026

What we collect

We collect information you choose to give us directly — for example, when you message us on WhatsApp to place an order or ask a question, or when you fill in the contact or newsletter forms on this site. This may include your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.

What we don't collect

This site has no user accounts, no analytics or advertising trackers, and no server-side database. We don't sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your cart and wishlist

Items you add to your cart or wishlist are stored only in your own browser (using local storage), so we can remember them between visits. This data never leaves your device or reaches us until you actually send an order through WhatsApp.

Contact and newsletter forms

The forms on our Contact and newsletter signup pages currently confirm submission in your browser but are not yet connected to an email inbox or mailing list — nothing you type there is transmitted or stored by us. If that changes, we'll update this policy.

Orders and WhatsApp

Ordering happens through WhatsApp, which is operated by Meta and governed by its own privacy policy. Any information you share with us there is used only to fulfil your order — confirm availability, arrange payment, and ship your items.

Third-party services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may see your IP address when the page loads, as is standard for any site using a font CDN. We don't use any other third-party embeds or scripts.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time what information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by messaging us via Contact or WhatsApp.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out through our Contact page.
