Frequently Asked Questions

Everything we get asked most often — if yours isn't here, just message us.

How do I place an order?

Add pieces to your cart and tap "Order via WhatsApp" — this opens a chat pre-filled with your order details so we can confirm availability, final pricing, and shipping with you directly.

Why does my piece look slightly different from the photo?

Every item is handmade from natural bamboo, so gentle variation in grain, tone, and weave is normal — it's part of what makes each piece one of a kind, not a defect.

How long does shipping take?

Most pieces are handcrafted and packed within 2–4 business days, then shipped pan-India via courier, typically arriving within 5–10 business days depending on your location. See our Shipping & Returns page for details.

How do I pay for my order?

Payment is arranged directly over WhatsApp once your order is confirmed — we'll share available options (such as UPI or cash on delivery, where offered) at that point.

Can I return or exchange a piece?

Yes — damaged or defective items can be returned or exchanged within 7 days of delivery. Full details are on our Shipping & Returns page.

How do I check the status of my order?

We don't yet have an automated tracking page — the fastest way is to message us on WhatsApp with your order details. See Order Tracking for how.

How should I care for my bamboo piece?

Wipe clean with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid prolonged soaking or direct exposure to heavy rain or intense sun, which can affect the natural finish over time.

Do you take bulk or custom orders?

We'd love to talk through it — reach out via our Contact page or WhatsApp with what you have in mind.
